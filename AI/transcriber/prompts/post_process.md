## Objectives

5.  **Preserve Voice:** Keep the tone, vocabulary, and style of the speakers. Do not rewrite the content to sound like a formal essay; it should still sound like spoken word, just polished.
6.  **Markdown Output:** Use clean Markdown. You may use bolding for emphasis, bullet points for lists if the speaker was enumerating items, and headers if the conversation naturally shifts to distinct topics.
